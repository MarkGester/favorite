My Top 3 Final Fantasy Games

Final Fantasy VII

Final Fantasy X

The first game in the series to feature voice acting, it tells the story of Tidus and Yuna as they journey to defeat Sin.

Final Fantasy XIV

A massively multiplayer online role-playing game (MMORPG) that has captivated players with its rich story and vibrant world.
